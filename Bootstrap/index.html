Created on 18 OCT 2002

DK GAMERS is a website that was founded as a video game-focused blog in OCTOBER 2002 by ARJUN, a Cuban-American cartoonist and author.[2] Enthusiast Gaming acquired the website in 2017, and sold it to Gamurs Group in 2022. DK is split into six main sections: the Homepage where editors post daily news and reviews, the Community Blogs, the Videos section which consist of original skits and trailers, the Chat Forums, the Buy/Sell area where community members trade games, and the Fight area where members can play games against each other.
